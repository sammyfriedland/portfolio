Universities to Attend

University of Illinois, Chicago (UIC)

UIC is appealing to me for many reasons, such as it is in my city, Chicago, so I would not be required to move long distances. Another reason I would like to attend UIC is beacuse it offers fantastic courses for aspiring graphic designers, which I would enjoy doing for a living. The tuition price is fairly cheap, at only $11,000 average tution after aid. It also includes a recreational facility, a sports facility, and an outdoor facility, as well as free WiFi for guests.

Columbia College University

Columbia College University is appealing for similar reasons to UIC. It is nearby, in Chicago, so not a lot of moving would be required. It has very good options for aspiring video game designers, being a media and art-based school. It has many amenitites, such as a computer lab, a fashion lab, a creative studio, and a book, paper, and printmaking facility.
